SIF Background and Interpretation

What is SIF?

SIF is a remote sensing index intended to monitor plant production

More conventional indices, such as NDVI or EVI, measure "greenness of vegetation" - a metric sometimes prone to producing erroneous results.

Curious? Click the button below to learn more!

What is the SIFVIZ map?

SIFVIZ is a map viewer displaying global seasonal shift in SIF.

Greater intensity of greens indicate greater seasonal change in chlorophyll fluorescence.

White flowing shapes demonstrate direction of increased chlorophyll concentration across seasons.
